Valuation of Barrick Gold + Newmont Goldcorp

Comprehensive financial analysis and valuation of major gold mining companies

School Project Financial Analysis, Excel, Bloomberg View Full Report

Project Overview

This comprehensive financial analysis project focused on determining the valuation of two of the world's largest gold mining companies: Barrick Gold and Newmont Goldcorp. The project required deep analysis of financial statements, market conditions, and commodity price trends to arrive at accurate company valuations.

Using professional financial tools and methodologies, I developed valuation models that incorporated both company-specific factors and broader market dynamics in the precious metals sector.

Analysis Methodology

Financial Statement Analysis

Comprehensive review of income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements for both companies over multiple years.

Ratio Analysis

Calculated and analyzed key financial ratios including profitability, liquidity, leverage, and efficiency metrics.

Commodity Price Projection

Developed projections for gold and copper prices based on market trends, supply-demand dynamics, and economic indicators.

Bloomberg Terminal Research

Utilized Bloomberg Terminal for real-time market data, peer comparisons, and industry analysis.

Key Analysis Components

  • Company Fundamentals: Analyzed production capacity, reserve quality, and operational efficiency
  • Market Position: Evaluated competitive advantages and market share in key geographic regions
  • Cost Structure: Examined all-in sustaining costs (AISC) and capital expenditure requirements
  • Risk Assessment: Identified geopolitical, operational, and commodity price risks
  • Valuation Models: Applied DCF, comparable company analysis, and asset-based valuation methods

Tools & Techniques

Excel Modeling

Built comprehensive financial models with sensitivity analysis and scenario planning capabilities.

Bloomberg Terminal

Professional-grade financial data platform for market research and analysis.

Valuation Methods

DCF analysis, P/E multiples, EV/EBITDA comparisons, and net asset value calculations.

Key Insights

The analysis revealed important insights about the relative valuations of these mining giants, considering factors such as:

  • Impact of gold price volatility on company valuations
  • Importance of operational efficiency in the mining sector
  • Role of geopolitical risk in asset valuation
  • Significance of reserve quality and mine life in long-term value
